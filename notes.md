## Graph Laplacian
Let $A$ --- graph adjacency matrix. \
Let $D$ --- graph degree matrix, that means $D = (\deg(v_1), \ldots, \deg(v_n))$.
**Graph Laplacian matrix** is defined by the formula
$$L = D - A.$$
